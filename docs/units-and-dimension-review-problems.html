Topic 9 Units and Dimension Review Problems

9.1 Units

A car is going 50 miles per hour. How many feet per second is it traveling? How many centimeters per second is it traveling? A sample of calcium nitrate Ca(NO2)3 contains 5 x 1025 oxygen atoms. How many kilograms of Calcium Nitrate are present? It will be helpful to know that 1 mole = 6.02 x 1023 molecules, and that 1 mole of calcium nitrate weighs 164 grams. Finally, one molecule of calcium nitrate contains 6 oxygen molecules.

9.2 Dimensional Analysis

A mixer with power P turns a mixer with wings of length D through a liquid of viscosity H. Find a formula for the angular velocity A = A(P,D,H). Since angles are dimensionless, we have [A] = 1/T. The dimensions of viscosity are [H] = M/(LT). The velocity V of a large ocean wave depends upon the period P, the acceleration of gravity g, and the density D of the water. Find a formula for the velocity V=V(P,g,D).
